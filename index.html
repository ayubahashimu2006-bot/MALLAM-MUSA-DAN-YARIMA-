MALLAM MUSA DAN YARIMA

ASSALAMUALAIKUM

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim

Muna mika sakon gaisuwa ga dukkan masu neman taimako da shiriya cikin alheri.

Muna bayar da hidima ta musamman wacce ta shafi karatun Al-Qur’ani mai girma da kuma shawarwari na rayuwa bisa hikima da fahimta. Aikinmu ya ta’allaka ne da taimakon mutane cikin halattattun hanyoyi masu tsafta ba tare da amfani da abubuwan da suka saba wa addini ba.

Ayyukan da muke bayarwa sun hada da:

  1. Neman arziki da samun sauƙin rayuwa
  2. Shawarwarin aure da zaman iyali
  3. Taimako wajen samun nasara a harkokin rayuwa da aiki
  4. Jagoranci kan yanke hukunci (istikhara)
  5. Taimako ga masu fuskantar matsalolin rayuwa daban-daban
  6. Karfafa imani da fahimtar Al-Qur’ani
  7. Taimako ga dalibai wajen karatu da jarrabawa
  8. Neman zaman lafiya da kwanciyar hankali a zuciya da gida

Muna aiki ne da nufin kawo haske da kwanciyar hankali ga rayuwar mutane ta hanyar shiriya, addu’a, da fahimtar addini.

Domin karin bayani ko neman taimako na musamman, za a iya tuntubar mu kai tsaye ta hanyar da aka tanada.
